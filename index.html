The Heritage

Songjin — the "Crown of Brocades"

Originating in the Song Dynasty (960–1279), Songjin is renowned for its two-warp, three-weft structure, creating a fabric that is lustrous, durable, and wrinkle-resistant.

Each accessory is crafted from 100% natural mulberry silk, dyed with traditional plant-based methods. We honor this living heritage by bringing it into daily life — so you can wear a piece of history.
